Earnings & Withdrawal Rules

💬 Invite Friends

Invite someone to the platform and when they register and upload music, you earn MK 300 per invited user.

👍 Likes

Each like on your uploaded song earns you MK 5.

📥 Downloads

Each download of your song gives you MK 7.

👁 Views

Every 3 views = MK 2. So, for every 3 people who play your song, you earn money.

💸 Withdraw Minimum

You can withdraw your balance when it reaches MK 50,000. You’ll receive a message after the admin approves your request.
